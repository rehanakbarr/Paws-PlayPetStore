About Us

Our Mission

At Paws & Play Pet Store, we believe every pet deserves the best care and love. Founded in 2015, we've been serving our community with premium pet supplies, expert advice, and compassionate service.

Our experienced team of pet lovers is dedicated to helping you provide the best possible care for your furry, feathered, or scaled family members. From nutrition counseling to grooming services, we're here to support you every step of the way.

We pride ourselves on carrying only the highest quality products from trusted brands, ensuring your pets get the nutrition, toys, and care they deserve to live happy, healthy lives.

Our Services

Professional Grooming

Full-service grooming including baths, haircuts, nail trimming, and dental care to keep your pets looking and feeling their best.

Veterinary Clinic

On-site veterinary services with experienced vets providing check-ups, vaccinations, and medical care for your beloved pets.

Training Classes

Professional training programs for dogs of all ages, from puppy socialization to advanced obedience and behavior modification.

Doggy Daycare

Safe and fun daycare services where your dogs can socialize, play, and exercise while you're away from home.
